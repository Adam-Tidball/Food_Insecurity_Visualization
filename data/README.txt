# Food Insecurity Visualization Data

This data folder contains datasets related to food insecurity in Canada. 
The data has been sourced from Statistics Canada and covers the time period from 2018 to 2022.

## Data Sources

The colmplete dataset found in this folder has been collected from the following sources:

1. Health characteristics, annual estimates (https://www150.statcan.gc.ca/t1/tbl1/en/tv.action?pid=1310009601&pickMembers%5B0%5D=1.1&pickMembers%5B1%5D=2.2&pickMembers%5B2%5D=3.1&cubeTimeFrame.startYear=2015&cubeTimeFrame.endYear=2022&referencePeriods=20150101%2C20220101)
2. Food insecurity by selected demographic characteristics (https://www150.statcan.gc.ca/t1/tbl1/en/tv.action?pid=1310083501&pickMembers%5B0%5D=1.1&cubeTimeFrame.startYear=2018&cubeTimeFrame.endYear=2022&referencePeriods=20180101%2C20220101)
3. Consumer Price Index (CPI) statistics, measures of core inflation and other related statistics - Bank of Canada definitions (https://www150.statcan.gc.ca/t1/tbl1/en/tv.action?pid=1810025601)

Please refer to the respective sources for more detailed information about the data collection methodology and any specific terms of use.

## Data Files

The following files are included in this data folder:

- CPI_metrics_Canada: 11 Different CPI metrics for every month from 2018 to 2022 (Canada as a whole, no individual provinces) 
- Food_Insecuirty_Demographics: Many different levels of food insecurity for different age catgeories, and other demographics. Every year from 2018 to 2022 (Canada & provinces)
- Food_Insecuirty_Less_Demographics: Only one level of food insecurit for all ages in Canada. Every year from 2018 to 2022 (Canada & provinces)
- Health_Characteristics: Just over 30 health characteristics for all people 12 years of age or older. Every year from 2018 to 2022 (Canada & provinces)
